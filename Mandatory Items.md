*From section 2.5.2.1 in SPINS*

The following items will be carried on the trainee’s person at all times:

1. Identification Card. Government issued photo ID (e.g., Common Access Card, Driver’s License, Passport, etc.)
   
2. Hydration System.
   
3. Writing Implement/Material. Pencil or blue or black ink pen, paper to take notes. 
   Exceptions: Not required for PT sessions.
   
4. Two properly filled out copies of the [[AETC Form 341]] IAW Attachment 6.
   
5. Reflective Belt. See Chapter 3 for approved deviations.
   
6. _OTS SPINS_.
   
7. Two (2) copies of your orders

NOTES:
- USE BUDDY SYSTEM TO STAY ACCOUNTABLE FOR YOUR MANDATORY ITEMS
- NEVER ASSUME YOU HAVE EVERYTHING
