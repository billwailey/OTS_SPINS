*From section 2.4.3 in SPINS*

When any staff member addresses an Officer Trainee, the Officer Trainee will respond with one of the following seven basic responses, unless otherwise directed. For situations not specified, Officer Trainees will begin or end every sentence with Sir/Ma’am.

1. “Yes, Sir/Ma’am.”

2. “No, Sir/Ma’am.”

3. “Sir/Ma’am, I do not know.”

4. “Sir/Ma’am, I do not understand.”

5. “Sir/Ma’am, may I ask a question?”

6. “Sir/Ma’am, may I make a statement?”

7. “Sir/Ma’am, the answer is.”
